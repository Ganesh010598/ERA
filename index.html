About Era Dance Academy

Welcome to
Era Dance Academy

Endless Rhythm Alaigal (ERA) DANCE ACADEMY was established in January 1992 in Klang. The group has grown in strength and spread its wings throughout Malaysia. The ERA dance academy is one of the most leading dance academies in Malaysia and they are well known for all kinds of Asian and international dance.

  • ERA is a role model for all the groups in Malaysia.

  • The dance academy is enhanced with new ideas.

  • A group with a long-term vision.
